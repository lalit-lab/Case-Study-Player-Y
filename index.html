Key observations: • Numerical advantage available • Site B control available • Bomb carrier delayed site commitment • Execution timing reduced round conversion probability

Afterward, the teammate positioned on A side was eliminated, confirming enemy presence toward A. Despite this information, Player X rotated aggressively toward A through exposed positions while running. The movement produced audio cues and revealed rotational timing. At 1:15, Player X was eliminated near Top Mid. Key observations: • Enemy A-side presence confirmed after teammate death • Rotation performed through exposed path • Running movement revealed audio information • Elimination occurred before site access or trade setup

The elimination occurred immediately after exiting the smoke without trade support or confirmed Mid control. Key observations: • No confirmed Mid control before smoke crossing • Push performed without teammate trade setup
